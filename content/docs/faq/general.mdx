## General FAQ

Common questions about Moydus services and how we work.

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I get started with Moydus?**
A: You can start by [browsing our packages](https://app.moydus.com), [viewing templates](https://moydus.com/marketplace/templates), or [contacting us](https://moydus.com/contact) for a consultation.

**Q: Do you offer free consultations?**
A: Yes, we offer free initial consultations to discuss your project needs and requirements.

**Q: How long does a typical project take?**
A: Project timelines vary:
- Simple websites: 2-4 weeks
- E-commerce platforms: 4-8 weeks
- Custom SaaS: 8-16 weeks
- Enterprise solutions: 12+ weeks

### Services

**Q: What services does Moydus offer?**
A: We offer web development, SaaS development, automation services, UI/UX design, marketing & SEO, and panel management solutions.

**Q: Do you provide ongoing support?**
A: Yes, we offer various support plans including basic email support, premium 24/7 support, and maintenance plans.

**Q: Can I customize packages?**
A: Absolutely! All packages are customizable. You can [create your own package](https://app.moydus.com) or contact us for custom solutions.

### Pricing

**Q: What are your pricing models?**
A: We offer fixed-price packages, custom quotes for enterprise projects, and subscription-based services for ongoing support.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**
A: Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for larger projects. Contact us to discuss options.

**Q: Are there any hidden costs?**
A: No hidden costs. All pricing is transparent and discussed upfront before project start.

### Process

**Q: What is your development process?**
A: Our process includes consultation, proposal, development, testing, delivery, and support. Learn more in our [process documentation](/docs/processes/development).

**Q: Will I be involved during development?**
A: Yes, we maintain regular communication and involve you in key decision points throughout the project.

**Q: Do you provide project updates?**
A: Yes, we provide regular updates via your preferred communication method (email, Slack, etc.).
