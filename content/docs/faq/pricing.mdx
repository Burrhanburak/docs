## Pricing FAQ

Common questions about Moydus pricing and payment options.

<Callout type="warn">
  **Transparent Pricing** - All costs are discussed upfront with no hidden fees. We provide detailed quotes before starting any project, and prices are fixed once agreed upon. Scope changes may result in additional costs, which we always discuss beforehand.

**Q: Do you offer free consultations?**
A: Yes, we offer free initial consultations to discuss your project and provide estimates.

**Q: Are there any hidden costs?**
A: No, all costs are discussed upfront. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

### Payment Options

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and other payment methods. Contact us for details.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**
A: Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for larger projects. Typically, payments are split across project milestones.

**Q: When is payment due?**
A: Payment terms vary by project. Usually, we require a deposit to start, with remaining payments at milestones.

### Package Pricing

**Q: Can I customize packages?**
A: Yes, all packages are customizable. You can add or remove features to fit your budget and needs.

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
A: We may offer discounts for long-term projects, multiple projects, or annual contracts. Contact us to discuss.

**Q: What's included in package pricing?**
A: Package details vary, but typically include development, design, testing, deployment, and initial support. Check individual package pages for details.

### Custom Projects

**Q: How do you price custom projects?**
A: Custom projects are priced based on requirements, complexity, timeline, and resources needed. We provide detailed quotes after consultation.

**Q: Can I get a quote before starting?**
A: Yes, we provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements. No obligation to proceed.

**Q: Do prices change during development?**
A: Prices are fixed once agreed upon. Changes to scope may result in additional costs, which we discuss before implementation.

### Ongoing Costs

**Q: Are there ongoing costs?**
A: Ongoing costs may include hosting, maintenance, and support. These are optional and discussed upfront.

**Q: What about maintenance costs?**
A: Maintenance costs vary based on the plan you choose. We offer various maintenance packages to fit different needs.

**Q: Do you charge for updates?**
A: Minor updates may be included in support plans. Major updates or new features are quoted separately.

### Refunds & Cancellations

**Q: What is your refund policy?**
A: Refund policies vary by project type. We discuss this during the initial consultation and include it in contracts.

**Q: Can I cancel a project?**
A: Yes, projects can be cancelled. Terms depend on project stage and are outlined in our agreements.
