## How Moydus Works

Understanding our process helps you know what to expect when working with Moydus.

<Callout type="info">
  **Timeline Estimates** - Project timelines are estimates and may vary based on project complexity, client feedback cycles, and scope changes. We provide more accurate timelines after the initial consultation and requirements gathering phase.
</Callout>

### Our Process Overview

1. **Consultation** - We discuss your needs and requirements
2. **Proposal** - We create a customized proposal for your project
3. **Development** - Our team builds your solution
4. **Testing** - Quality assurance and testing
5. **Delivery** - Launch and handover
6. **Support** - Ongoing maintenance and support

### Timeline

Project timelines vary based on complexity:

- **Simple Websites**: 2-4 weeks
- **E-commerce Platforms**: 4-8 weeks
- **Custom SaaS**: 8-16 weeks
- **Enterprise Solutions**: 12+ weeks
