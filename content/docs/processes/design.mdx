## Design Process

Our design process focuses on creating intuitive, beautiful, and user-friendly interfaces.

<Callout type="info">
  **User-Centered Approach** - We prioritize user needs and goals in every design decision. Our designs are tested with real users to ensure they're intuitive and effective before development begins.
</Callout>

### Design Stages

1. **Research & Discovery**
   - User research
   - Competitor analysis
   - Brand identity review
   - User personas

2. **Wireframing**
   - Information architecture
   - User flow mapping
   - Low-fidelity wireframes
   - Structure validation

3. **Visual Design**
   - Color palette selection
   - Typography choices
   - Icon design
   - High-fidelity mockups

4. **Prototyping**
   - Interactive prototypes
   - User testing
   - Iteration and refinement
   - Final design approval

5. **Design Handoff**
   - Design specifications
   - Asset preparation
   - Developer collaboration
   - Style guide creation

### Design Principles

- **User-Centered**: Focus on user needs and goals
- **Accessibility**: WCAG compliant designs
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first approach
- **Consistent**: Cohesive brand experience
